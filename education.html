Understanding Mental Health

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act as we cope with life. Learning about mental health is crucial for prevention, understanding, and management of mental conditions.

Curriculum Integration

Integrating mental health education into school curriculums is vital for early awareness and intervention. Schools play a critical role in educating young people about mental health alongside academic skills.

Professional Development for Teachers

Training for teachers on how to address and support mental health in educational settings is crucial. It helps educators identify early signs of mental health issues and provide appropriate support to students.
